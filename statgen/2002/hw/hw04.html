Problem set 4: Interval mapping

  1. Implement (in R) the EM algorithm for interval mapping with a single-QTL model, under the assumption that the residual variation is homoscedastic and normally distributed, for a backcross. Consider just one pair of markers at a recombination fraction, r; assuming complete data at the markers and no crossover interference.

  2. Try out the R/qtl software. Follow along the tutorial. You might even look at the code.
